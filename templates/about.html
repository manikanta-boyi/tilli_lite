Our Vision: Unified Enterprise Solutions

Tilli-Lite is an innovative, single-platform architecture designed to streamline customer interactions, financial management, and communication across your enterprise.

tX

tilliX - The Customer Portal

tilliX provides a robust, self-service dashboard where customers can manage their accounts, view usage data, and interact with support resources. It is the central hub for the customer journey, ensuring full transparency and control over their services.

M

Monay - Integrated Payments & Billing

Monay handles all aspects of billing and payment processing. Seamlessly integrated into tilliX, it offers secure, flexible payment options and clear bill presentation, drastically improving payment success rates and customer satisfaction.

N

Nudge - Personalized Communications

Nudge is our intelligent communication layer. It uses customer data from tilliX and Monay to send personalized, timely, and relevant communications—from service updates and payment reminders to special offers—through preferred channels.
